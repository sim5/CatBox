Cat vs Dogs

  • High Concept: A cat fighting against dogs to decide who will rule the backyard.
  • Player Experience Goals: Careful play to dodge the dog bones and rule the dogs.
  • Genre: Shooting game.
  • Game play: Players can only move sideways using the arrow keys. Space bar shoots fish bones. Players have to dodge the dog bones in order to not lose the 3 given lives. If any dog crosses the screen border, game ends.
  • Prototype goal: shooting, dodging, moving.
  • Features: Multiple levels and increasing difficulty according to levels.
  • Setting/Premise: There is a house that a cat and many dogs live together. However, they do not like each other and they finally have decided to choose who will be the ruler of the backyard. And they began fight.
  • Story: The one and only cat and the dogs of a house met in backyard to determine who will be the ruler of this house.
  • Target Audience: Any age group can play this game.
  • Play time: Less than 5 minutes.
  • Strategies: It will be effective to estimate the movement of the dogs to shoot them.
